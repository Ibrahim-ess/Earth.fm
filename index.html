Earth.fm is an online experiment that seeks to protect and regenerate natural ecosystems while reconnecting us to nature. Our project is inspired by the Earth Holder Community created by Zen Master Thich Nhat Hanh, by the ethical NFT movement and the nouns project, and in equal measure by our love for podcasts.

We draw inspiration from many sources and from many inspiring beings around the globe, who use their time here enjoying the precious gift of life and at the same time protecting the more-than-human world.

“An Earth Holder* works to preserve this planet for living beings by taking care of the air, water, and soil.”

*The Bodhisattva Dharanimdhara - an enlightened being from ancient Buddhist texts — is translated by Zen Master Thich Nhat Hanh in his book “The World We Have” to be the Earth Holder.

How does it work ?

Every day we'll offer for auction an NFT “episode” composed of a one-hour natural soundscape and a painting inspired by it. In parallel, each episode will be uploaded daily to all podcast platforms, free for everyone to listen to.

We aim to use around 80% of our revenue to support various nature restoration and conservation charities chosen and voted on by the community, according to principles backed by science.
